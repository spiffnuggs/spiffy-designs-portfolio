   BERBER WAVES WEBSITE - HOW TO UPDATE YOUR CONTENT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Hi Adeline! Here's how to update your website without touching any code.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PART 1: CHANGING IMAGES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

All your images are in the "images" folder. To replace any image:

1. Go to the "images" folder
2. Delete the image you want to replace
3. Add your new image
4. Rename it EXACTLY the same as the one you deleted

That's it! The website will automatically show your new image.


YOUR IMAGE FILES:
─────────────────

  logo.png              → Your logo (top of page + footer)
  hero.jpg              → Big background image on homepage
  room-private.jpg      → Private room photo
  room-shared.jpg       → Shared dorm photo
  workspace.jpg         → Digital nomad / terrace photo
  community.jpg         → Friends / group photo
  activity-sandboarding.jpg → Sandboarding activity photo


TIPS FOR IMAGES:
────────────────
• Keep the same file extension (.jpg or .png)
• Recommended size: at least 1200px wide for best quality
• Smaller file sizes = faster loading (compress at tinypng.com)

PART 2: CHANGING TEXT & PRICES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

To change text, prices, or contact info:

1. Open "index.html" in any text editor (TextEdit, Notepad, etc.)
2. Look for this section near the bottom (search for "EDIT YOUR CONTENT"):

   // =============================================
   // EDIT YOUR CONTENT HERE
   // =============================================

3. Change ONLY the text between "quotes"
4. Save the file

EXAMPLE - Changing a price:
───────────────────────────

  Before:  price: "€299",
  After:   price: "€349",


WHAT YOU CAN CHANGE:
────────────────────

HERO SECTION:
  • tagline (the small text above title)
  • title (main headline)
  • subtitle (description below title)
  • buttonText (the booking button)

PACKAGES (you have 3):
  • title
  • description
  • price
  • tag (like "POPULAR" or "BEST VALUE")

ROOMS (you have 2):
  • name
  • price

REVIEWS (you have 2):
  • name
  • text

CONTACT INFO:
  • phone
  • email
  • whatsapp (just the numbers, no spaces)
  • instagram link
  • facebook link


⚠️  IMPORTANT - DON'T BREAK IT:
────────────────────────────────
• Only change text BETWEEN the "quotes"
• Don't delete any commas, brackets, or quotes
• Don't edit anything below "END OF EDITABLE CONTENT"
