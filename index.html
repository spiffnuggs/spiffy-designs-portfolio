About

Built businesses. Then built the tech.

I'm Sydney — most people call me Spiffy. I've started businesses from scratch, scaled them, and learned something the hard way every single time: you can have the best product in the world, but if your website doesn't do it justice, none of it matters.

So I learned to build. And then I couldn't stop.

Now I build for other business owners who are exactly where I've been — buried in admin, duct-taping tools together, spending more time on the stuff they hate than the work they actually love. I find the right solution, build it custom, and hand back their time. More customers served. More hours with family and friends. Less of the work that makes you want to throw your laptop.
