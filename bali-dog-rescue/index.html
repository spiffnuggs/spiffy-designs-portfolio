Our Mission

A Home for the Forgotten Dogs of Bali

Bali's streets are full of dogs that have been abandoned, injured, or born into hardship. What started as one man helping one dog has grown into a full-time rescue operation caring for over 50 dogs.

Every dog gets veterinary care, proper nutrition, shelter, and — most importantly — love. Our goal is to rehabilitate and rehome as many as we can, while providing a permanent sanctuary for those who need it.

"I came to Bali for a new life. I didn't expect to find my purpose in the eyes of a street dog."
